Eagle

Eagles are birds of prey in the family Accipitridae; there are approximately 60 different species. The majority are found in Eurasia and Africa, with only 14 species found in other areas including North, Central and South America, and Australia.

Liger

Ligers, believed to be fictional creatures by some, are very real and very rare hybrid species only found in captivity. Ligers are born to a female Tiger (Panthera tigris) mother and a lion (Panthera leo) father.

Reindeer

Reindeer (Rangifer tarandus) belongs to the species Cervidae. The species is a natural part of Northern ecosystems and is resident in the northern parts of Europe, Asia and North America.
